Podcast Script Producer

Problem Statement: Creating a podcast script that sounds natural for multiple personas requires significant research, planning, and back-and-forth editing. Doing this manually for each episode is repetitive and slow, especially when balancing different guest voices and perspectives.

Expected Outcomes:

Given a topic and two personas, the system produces a full interview-style script
Each guest's responses are clearly distinct in tone and voice
Human can review and rewrite any segment before the next one is generated
Production can be paused and resumed across multiple sessions without losing progress
